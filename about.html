About DevCircle

We're a passionate team of web designers, developers, and digital marketing experts based in Nairobi CBD, dedicated to transforming your digital presence.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate tech entrepreneurs, DevCircle emerged from a shared vision to bridge the digital gap for businesses across Kenya. What started as a small team working from a co-working space in Nairobi CBD has grown into a leading digital agency.

We've had the privilege of working with over 200+ clients, from startups to established enterprises, helping them establish a strong digital presence and achieve their business goals through innovative web solutions.

Our name "DevCircle" represents the collaborative circle of developers, designers, and strategists who come together to create exceptional digital experiences.
